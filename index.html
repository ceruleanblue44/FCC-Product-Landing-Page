Construction

We make our ukuleles of acacia koa. Acacia koa is a species of flowering tree in the pea family, Fabaceae. It is endemic to the Hawaiian Islands, where it is the second most common tree. The highest populations are on Hawaiʻi, Maui and Oʻahu. It's name in the Hawaiian language, koa, means brave, bold, fearless, or warrior. Koa is a tonewood, it possesses tonal properties that make it ideal for use in acoustic stringed instruments.
